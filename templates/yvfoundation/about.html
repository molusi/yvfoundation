About

YV Foundation is a non-profit organization which was established in 2020 by a group of 4 young women aged “between” 23 to 34, who want to help with youth development and raise awareness around Gender Based Violence in mainly disadvantaged communities in South Africa. The organization addresses social issues such as unemployment and gender based violence.
